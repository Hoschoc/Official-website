Quality Assurance

       The management strives for perfection in production procedure and quality control assurance in order to give clients our all-out effort in this intense market.

  • All raw materials purchased from the top 500 suppliers, such as SUN CHEMICAL Engelhard, strahl&pitseh, Merch, Kingfisher, Kahl, CRODA…etc.
  • All materials we purchase will get through the TQC, and stored at proper temperature and humidity to avoid any metamorphism.
  • All our staff are trained strictly to ensure the quality control in each production station.
  • All the raw materials, semi-products and finished products must undergo a variety of tests.
  • Outsourced suppliers must pass our quality evaluation and tests to ensure all delivery products are in qualified/good condition.

    • Quality Assurance

    Quality Control

    IQC (In-coming Quality Control)
    Quality inspection for in-coming raw materials which are used to make products

    IPQC (In-Process Quality Control)
    QC content inspection、Stability / compatibility test、heavy metal test、microbial test、and antimicrobial protection test are performed in the manufacturing steps to assure the safety of the products.

    FQC (Finished Product Quality Control)
    FQC is performed to avoid reject products are released to customers. This could protect the credibility of our enterprise and customers.

    Quality Management

    Quality Maintenance
    Work for product quality performance requirements, including customer、law、regulation requirements.

    Instrument Calibration
    Instrument calibration is performed periodically to assure the accuracy of instrument measurement and inspection quality.

    Environmental maintenance
    Follow the relevant building laws and regulations and maintain the appropriate and adequate distance to avoid pollution and fire.
    Disinfect workplaces, air filtration systems and manufacturing equipment and equipment periodically. Keep the environment clean and ventilated. Implement health management thoroughly.
    Quality Control and managemt follow the requirements of ISO 22716:2007 (Cosmetics -- Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP) -- Guidelines on Good Manufacturing Practices).

Quality Assurance

品質保證

Quality Assurance

Quality Control

IQC (In-coming Quality Control)

Quality inspection for in-coming raw materials which are used to make products.

IPQC (In-Process Quality Control)

QC content inspection、Stability / compatibility test、heavy metal test、microbial test、and antimicrobial protection test are performed in the manufacturing steps to assure the safety of the products.

FQC (Finished Product Quality Control)

FQC is performed to avoid reject products are released to customers. This could protect the credibility of our enterprise and customers.

Quality Management

Quality Maintenance

Work for product quality performance requirements, including customer、law、regulation requirements.

Instrument Calibration

Instrument calibration is performed periodically to assure the accuracy of instrument measurement and inspection quality.

Environmental Maintenance

Follow the relevant building laws and regulations and maintain the appropriate and adequate distance to avoid pollution and fire. Disinfect workplaces, air filtration systems and manufacturing equipment and equipment periodically. Keep the environment clean and ventilated. Implement health management thoroughly. Quality Control and managemt follow the requirements of ISO 22716:2007 (Cosmetics -- Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP) -- Guidelines on Good Manufacturing Practices).

品質保證

品質控管

進料檢驗

進貨的原料經由嚴密的檢查加以確認品質。

製程檢驗

產品生產階段作安定性、安全性檢測、重金屬檢測、及微生物防腐效能試驗，確保產品品質安全無虞。

成品檢驗

防止不合格品流到用戶手中，避免對用戶造成損失，也是為了保護企業的信譽。

品質管理

品質維護

致力於品質要求，以達到產品的固有特性滿足顧客、法律、法規等方面所提出的品質要求。

儀器校驗

定期對其使用儀器進行校正確保檢驗品質。

環境維護

依建築相關法規，並與周圍邊界保持足以避免污染及防火需要之適當距離。定期消毒作業場所、空氣過濾系統及製造分裝設備與器具，保持環境潔淨與通風，徹底執行衛生管理。工廠現場管制及管理皆依據國際化粧品法規「化粧品優良製造規範」(ISO 22716:2007)。

Company Profile

Hoschoc Enterprise Co., Ltd.

Hoschoc is a brand-new cosmetics manufacturing company stemmed from the success of long-standing Mei Shual Cosmetics Co., Ltd. The first generation founded Mei Shual, the second generation engineered the achievement of over 10 thousand products exported to Europe, America, and Japan.
Now, helmed by the mastermind behind this success, the third generation brought the legacy together with a new innovative and dynamic team to launch Hoschoc Enterprise Co., Ltd. in Tainan, Taiwan in 2016.

公司簡介

好思佳粧業股份有限公司

好思佳的誕生，源自於美帥彩妝代工廠的擴大經營理念，在擁有40年行銷世界的經驗下，產品外銷至歐、美、日等名牌，超過10000多項美妝產品的成績。而今為了讓台灣化妝品的經驗傳承與創新，在長輩的帶領之下由家族第三代獨立在外經營好思佳美妝代工廠，於2016年開始設定在安平工業區建廠，並開始籌組創新活力的工作團隊。

Missions

The expert Hoschoc team dares to be bold and delivers unique products. Being professional, diligent, and sincere is the core of our value.
  • Short-term goal: To create a pleasant working environment that is GMP compliant, enhance efficiency and stabilize quality to add value to our customers.
  • Mid-term goal: To strengthen company reputation by committing to the well-being of our employees and relationships with our suppliers, and extending it to our customers and the community.
  • Long-term goal: To become a key player in the cosmetics manufacturing industry and facilitate Taiwan Excellence into the world.

    • Facility and Equipment

    The 3,960m2 factory is designed with GMP compliance and is expected to be GMP and ISO certified by Q3 of 2017. 1500m2 of office and storage space is built with the needs of market development and design and the storage security of products and confidential formulas.
    The factory is equipped with powder, ointment, and emulsion manufacturing, dry and wet powder pressing, lipstick and liquid filling, and R&D laboratory facilities.

    設備規劃

    廠房：1500坪廠房是以GMP製造精神做設計，將會在創立2017年第三季完成GMP與ISO等國際認證。400坪辦公室及倉庫則以市場開發與設計人員需求為設計，並以安全存放成品與配方等機密需求為設計。
    設備：分為電腦系統及生產系統，電腦系統為鼎新ERP系統輔助，將以管理角度打造智慧工廠提升生產效率。另生產系統，就粉類製造、油膏類製造、乳化類製造，分別備有調製配方相關設備、壓製乾粉產品設備、壓製濕粉產品設備、灌製口紅產品設備、灌製液態產品設備、實驗與研發相關設備，設備規劃上以近千萬元價值的機台設備於廠內生產製造。

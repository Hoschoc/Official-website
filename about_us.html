Company Profile

Hoschoc Enterprise Co., Ltd.

      Hoschoc is a brand-new cosmetics manufacturing company. We have own cosmetic factory in Taiwan. We are experienced in supplying a wide range of cosmetic products at different levels and prices that satisfies every expectation of our customers.
      We actively seek partners in various aspects from product design to its components, accessories, and packaging. We offer cosmetic Original Equipment Manufacturer(OEM) & Original Design Manufacturer(ODM) and custom productions at the highest standard to meet the needs of our customers.

Facility and Equipment

      The 3,960m2 factory is designed with GMP compliance and is expected to be GMP and ISO certified by Q4 of 2017. 1500m2 of office and storage space is built with the needs of market development and design and the storage security of products and confidential formulas.
      The cosmetic factory is equipped with powder, ointment, and emulsion manufacturing, dry and wet powder pressing, lipstick and liquid filling, and Research and Developmemt (R&D) laboratory facilities.

Missions

      The expert Hoschoc team dares to be bold and delivers unique products. Being professional, diligent, and sincere is the core of our value.
  • Short-term goal: To create a pleasant working environment that is GMP compliant, enhance efficiency and stabilize quality to add value to our customers.
  • Mid-term goal: To strengthen company reputation by committing to the well-being of our employees and relationships with our suppliers, and extending it to our customers and the community.
  • Long-term goal: To become a key player in the cosmetics manufacturing industry and facilitate Taiwan Excellence into the world.
